- A string of macro prints (CPI/PPI/FOMC) clustered in one window can manufacture a multi-session cash drag even when the regime itself permits deployment. The veto is legitimate, but the cost (full-week S&P lag) must be tracked, not waved away.

The judgment window is the rolling 60-day, and the diagnosis here is "nothing qualified + macro vetoes," which §13 calls examinable but not automatically a fault.
- Being flat is only "defended cash" if a qualifying pipeline is ready the moment vetoes lift. We were not pipeline-ready — that is the fixable gap.
